## Project Status
- **Stage:** Prototype (HTML/React)
- **Business Plan:** Complete (see Music-School-Business-Plan.docx)

## Pricing Model (from business plan)
- Solo: $19/mo (1 teacher, 30 students)
- Studio: $49/mo (5 teachers, 100 students)
- School: $99/mo (unlimited)
