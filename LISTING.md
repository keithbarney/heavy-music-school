# MusicDesk — Music School Management, Simplified

## Tagline
Everything your music school needs. Nothing it doesn't.

## Short Description
MusicDesk is a SaaS platform that helps music schools and independent teachers manage students, lessons, assignments, billing, and communication — all from one dashboard.

## The Problem
Music teachers spend hours every week on admin: chasing students for practice updates, juggling text messages from parents, manually tracking attendance, and printing sheet music. Schools lack visibility into teacher workloads and student retention. The tools that exist are clunky, expensive, or built for generic tutoring — not music.

## The Solution
MusicDesk is purpose-built for music education:

- **For Teachers:** Create rich assignments with embedded videos, sheet music, and tablature. See at a glance who's practiced and who needs attention. Set your availability and let students book.
- **For Students:** A simple to-do list of what to practice, with videos and sheet music right there. Track your streak. Earn badges. Submit recordings for feedback.
- **For Parents:** See what's assigned, what's done, and how your child is progressing. Book lessons, manage billing, and message the teacher — all in one place.
- **For School Admins:** Dashboard of school health metrics, teacher management, enrollment workflows, and reporting.

## Key Features
- 📅 **Scheduling** — Teacher availability, lesson booking, band rehearsals
- 📝 **Assignments** — Videos, sheet music, tabs, instructions in one place
- 📊 **Progress Tracking** — Streaks, completion rates, badges, parent visibility
- 💬 **Messaging** — Centralized teacher-student-parent communication
- 🎷 **Band Management** — Group rosters, shared schedules, ensemble assignments
- 💳 **Billing** — Invoices, payment history, plan management
- 👶 **Kid-Friendly Mode** — Simplified UI for young students with visual tasks and rewards
